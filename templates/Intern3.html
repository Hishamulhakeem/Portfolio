Project Demo

Mini Project 1: Web Development

“Ruals Rroots” is a web-based platform that connects farmers and consumers through a unified marketplace. It features a service section where farmers can easily rent various agricultural tools and machinery for short-term use. The product section offers consumers access to a wide range of fresh, farm-grown goods directly from rural producers. The platform aims to empower farmers, simplify access to farming resources, and promote sustainable rural development.

Mini Project 2: Doctor Appointment(CRUD Operation)

This project is a dynamic web-based platform that allows users to efficiently manage doctor appointment schedules. It enables patients to book, update, or cancel appointments while allowing doctors to organize their daily schedules with ease. The system ensures smooth interaction between patients and healthcare providers, enhancing the overall appointment management experience.

Mini Project 3: Smart Attendance System

This is a smart and efficient system to automatically mark student attendance by analyzing a classroom photo — typically captured from a CCTV camera or even a decent smartphone camera. The system identifies student faces, matches them with the dataset, and marks them as present or absent. It also generates detailed reports and can notify parents via WhatsApp if their child is absent.
